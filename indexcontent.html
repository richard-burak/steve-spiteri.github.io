    • What is Lux

DHT11 Humidity Sensor

+             3.3v Input
-              Ground
S              Signal to GPIO 4 (pin7)

Octopus Barometric Pressure Sensor

SC           IICClock to GPIO 3 (pin5)
SD           IICData to GPIO 2 (pin3)
V             3.3v input (but has a regulator that can accept 5v)
G             Ground

Photocell

1μF Capacitor is used.

A capacitor will charge at a rate variable with the amount of light hitting the photocell. The program will return the time the capacitor took to charge. That will be matched with a lux value to identify day, night, and dusk/dawn. Many freeware Android applications exist to measure lux.



Solar Panel
A resistor in series to protect the Pi to GPIO 27 (pin 13). Testing needs to be complete to determine which resistor is needed.
